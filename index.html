The Team

The Nuggz NFT was founded by our love for nuggets and our insatiable addiction to buying NFTs. 3 of us IRL friends, randomly started talking one day and the bright idea came up to try our hand at a nugget based NFT project just for the laughs.   2 months of planning, working long hours, learning from so many great people in the community and insanely funny and awesome artwork by @yeetpizza, here we are.   

We wanted a light hearted project with a relatively affordable entry point for most people. Grab a few for yourself or grab some to give away to friends. Use them as your PFP, your phone wall paper or anywhere you want. We just hope these Nuggz give you a little chuckle and a hungry feeling for nuggets every time you look at them.

We are all NFT collectors and enthusiasts and plan to be in the NFT space and community for the rest of our lives. I’m pretty certain that 99% of the proceeds from this sale will be going directly back into the NFT space, the other 1% will go to paying back all of our credit card debts and instant ramen for the next month.
